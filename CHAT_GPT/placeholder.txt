import openai
import os

#get openai key
openai.api_key = os.environ.get('CHAT_GPT')

#lecture_content will be replaced by the result of the output of speech to text
lecture_content = '''
Good morning everyone,

Today, we delve into the fascinating realm of artificial intelligence and explore its profound impact on society. As technology continues to evolve, AI has emerged as a driving force that is reshaping the way we live, work, and interact with the world around us. In this lecture, we will discuss the key concepts of AI, its applications, benefits, challenges, and the ethical considerations that arise as AI becomes increasingly integrated into our lives.

Definition and Scope of Artificial Intelligence:
Artificial Intelligence, or AI, refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding. AI encompasses a spectrum of technologies, from narrow AI (designed for specific tasks) to general AI (possessing human-like cognitive abilities).

Applications of AI:
AI's applications are far-reaching and diverse. From healthcare and finance to transportation and entertainment, AI is revolutionizing industries. In healthcare, AI aids in diagnosing diseases and analyzing medical images, enhancing patient care. In finance, algorithms enable predictive analytics and algorithmic trading. Self-driving cars and smart cities leverage AI to optimize transportation and urban planning.

Benefits of AI:
AI's potential benefits are substantial. It can automate repetitive tasks, improve accuracy, and enhance decision-making. Additionally, AI-driven data analysis can lead to insights that were previously unattainable. For example, AI is instrumental in drug discovery, speeding up research processes that might otherwise take years.

Challenges and Concerns:
However, the rise of AI brings forth challenges. Concerns about job displacement due to automation are valid, and it's crucial to reskill and upskill the workforce for AI-related roles. Ethical dilemmas, such as bias in AI algorithms and the potential for misuse, need to be addressed. Striking a balance between technological advancement and societal well-being is a critical challenge.

Ethical Considerations:
AI introduces ethical complexities that require careful consideration. As AI systems learn from data, they can inadvertently perpetuate biases present in that data. Transparent and accountable AI development practices are essential to ensure fairness and prevent discriminatory outcomes. Moreover, the potential for AI to exceed human intelligence raises ethical questions about control and responsibility.

Conclusion:
In conclusion, artificial intelligence is reshaping society in profound ways. Its potential to revolutionize industries, improve efficiency, and solve complex problems is unparalleled. However, we must approach the integration of AI with mindfulness, addressing ethical concerns and fostering responsible innovation. As we navigate the AI era, collaboration between technologists, policymakers, and ethicists will be crucial to harness its benefits while mitigating its risks.

Thank you for your attention, and I encourage you to explore further the dynamic world of artificial intelligence and its ever-evolving impact on our lives.

Please note that this is a sample lecture and can be customized further based on the depth and focus you'd like to emphasize within the topic of artificial intelligence and its impact on society.
'''

#create a prompt to send to chatgpt
prompt = "Can you please summarize the main ideas of this lecture:" + lecture_content

#receive response
def get_completion(prompt):
    response = openai.Completion.create(
    engine = "text-davinci-003",  # Specify the engine you want to use
    prompt = prompt,
    max_tokens = 500  # Specify the maximum number of tokens in the response
    )
    return response.choices[0].text.strip()

#the output
response = get_completion(prompt)
print(response)
